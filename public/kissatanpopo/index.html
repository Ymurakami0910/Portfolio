This is our story

Discover the Heart of Kissa Tanpopo

The name "Kissa" means "tea house" in Japanese, and "Tanpopo" translates to "dandelion." This name reflects our mission, to be a welcoming space where people come together to enjoy tasty drinks and desserts. Every cup of coffee tells a story, from picking the best beans to brewing the perfect cup.

When you walk into Kissa Tanpopo, you'll feel right at home. Our café has warm lighting and earthy colors that remind you of a beautiful Japanese garden. We offer a special menu with a variety of coffee drinks, each one carefully chosen to go well with our hand-made sweets and light snacks.
